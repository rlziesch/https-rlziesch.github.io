Why 'Decide My Day'?

Have you ever experienced Decison Fatigue? Wikipedia defines this phenomenon as "the deteriorating quality of decisions made by an individual after a long session of decision making". Essentially, decision fatigue is finding too many choices to be overwhelming and thus experiencing negative effects such as decision avoidance and impulse purchasing. In efforts to find a fun way to battle decision fatigue, we created "Decide My Day".
